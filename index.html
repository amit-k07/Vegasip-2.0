About Us

VEGASIP is a premium fruit juice brand crafted for people who love natural taste and refreshing experiences. Our juices come in a modern can format, making them easy to carry and perfect for everyday lifestyle.

Made with real fruit extracts and carefully selected ingredients, VEGASIP delivers authentic flavor without carbonation. Each sip is designed to be smooth, healthy, and satisfying.

From classic favourites like Orange and Mango to exciting upcoming flavours like Tamarind, Guava, Peach, and Watermelon — we bring innovation and taste together in every can.

Our mission is simple — to provide a refreshing, healthy alternative to sugary drinks while maintaining premium quality and great taste.
